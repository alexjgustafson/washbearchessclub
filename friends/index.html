Friends of the Washbears

Awry Brewing

Our host every Tuesday since July 2025.

awrybrewing.com

Finish Line Realty

Scott Hack of Finish Line Realty has donated his Middletown media & training facility for several Washbear events and other community groups.

louisvillerealestate.com

Chess Education Foundation

CEF hosts a tournament every Monday night and a free casual night every Wednesday. Many Washbear members frequent both events. They also host some Washbear Chess Club events.

chesseducation.org

PB&J Homeschool

Alex coaches a budding chess team for this homeschool co-op.

pbjhomeschool.org

Kentucky Chess Association

KCA is the official US Chess affiliate for Kentucky and allows Washbear Chess Club to post events on its calendar. They're a great resource to find chess events in all parts of the commonwealth.

kychess.org

Chess Earth

A chess community in Cincinnati, Ohio, Chess Earth also maintains one of the most complete calendars of chess events in Ohio, Indiana, and Kentucky. They include Washbear Chess Club events in their calendar.

chessearth.com
