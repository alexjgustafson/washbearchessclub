# Product Overview

Washbear Chess Club is a chess club website for Louisville, Kentucky, established in 2025. The site serves as the digital presence for a US Chess Federation affiliate club.

## Key Features

- Tournament information and registration
- Club event announcements and updates
- Email subscription system for member communications
- Photo galleries from past tournaments
- Club merchandise (t-shirts)
- Rulebook and club information

## Target Audience

- Local chess players in Louisville, KY
- Tournament participants
- Chess enthusiasts looking for organized play
- New members interested in joining the club

## Brand Identity

- Uses a raccoon mascot (raccoon with chess rook imagery)
- Casual, welcoming tone
- Focus on community and fun competitive play
- "Trash Panda Throwdown" tournament series branding
